1) Information We Collect

No personal information is collected directly by us. We do not run sign-ups, user accounts, comments, or contact forms.

Automatically collected technical data may be processed by our hosting/CDN provider (Amazon Web Services, “AWS”), such as:

  • IP address and approximate region,
  • Browser/OS/device type,
  • Pages requested and timestamps, and
  • Referrer URL (if any).

This data is standard for web delivery and appears in server/CDN logs. We do not combine it with other data to identify individuals.

Cookies: We use only essential/site-operation cookies where necessary (e.g., via AWS CloudFront). We do not deploy analytics or cross-site tracking cookies ourselves.

2) How We Use Your Information

  • Operate & secure the Site: deliver pages, mitigate abuse, and diagnose issues.
  • Aggregate insights: high-level, non-identifying traffic patterns to improve content/navigation.
  • Legal compliance: respond to lawful requests and enforce our Terms.

3) Third-Party Services & Disclosures

Hosting & CDN (AWS): Site files are stored on AWS (e.g., S3) and delivered via AWS CloudFront. Standard request data necessarily passes through AWS systems.

No third-party analytics: We don’t use Google Analytics or similar tools.

Disclosures: We don’t sell or rent personal data. We may disclose limited technical data if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and property.

5) Data Retention & Security

Technical logs are retained only as long as needed for operations/security and are rotated or deleted thereafter. We rely on industry-standard controls (e.g., HTTPS, firewalling) and AWS security features. No method is 100% secure; use the Site at your own risk.

6) International Users (GDPR/UK)

The Site operates from the United States (primarily for U.S. audiences) but is globally accessible. If GDPR/UK GDPR applies to your visit, we process technical data under the legal basis of legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) to operate and secure the Site, without profiling or marketing. You may request access/erasure of any personal data we may hold (typically limited to log entries) by contacting us below.
