This Privacy Policy governs data practices across every page and URL under the deeptaanshu.com domain and all subdomains (for example, www.deeptaanshu.com, sports.deeptaanshu.com, finance.deeptaanshu.com). If a page is not hosted under deeptaanshu.com or its subdomains, this Policy may not apply.

No personal information is collected directly by us. We do not run sign-ups, user accounts, comments, or contact forms.

Automatically collected technical data may be processed by our hosting/CDN provider (Amazon Web Services, “AWS”), such as IP address, browser/OS, pages requested, timestamps, and referrer. We do not combine these with other data to identify individuals.

Cookies: Only essential/operational cookies (if any) are used, e.g., via AWS CloudFront. We do not deploy our own analytics or cross-site tracking cookies.
